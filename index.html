Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the stump grinding process take?

The process typically takes 30 minutes to 2 hours, depending on the size and location of the stump.

Will stump grinding damage my lawn?

We take great care to minimize any impact on your lawn. Our equipment is designed to be low-impact, and we clean up thoroughly after the job is done.

What happens to the wood chips?

We can either remove the wood chips or leave them for you to use as mulch in your garden.
